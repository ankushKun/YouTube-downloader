For downloading video:
	use this command : video (link)
	example: video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
	resolution : low = 144p , medium = 360p , high = 720p

For downloading whole playlist:
	use this command : playlist (link of playlist) (no. of video playlist have)

For downloading audio:
	use this command : audio (link)
	ex: audio https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
